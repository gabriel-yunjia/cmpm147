Experiment 4 pt3 -Infinite Space

Description

What are you trying to acomplish with this experiment?
This is an infinite world with infinite numbers of planets, stars, and UFOs. You can scroll through the world to see its life and infinite beauty.


Technical

Describe in detail what you did technically and how it works?
I used hashmaps, random numbers, and the millis() function to create the stars and UFOS. The giant red planets as well as the UFO also move up and down using the millis() function. Sometimes the smaller planets have a chance of spawning with a ring. The stars generate a random amount of circles of various sizes within each tile.
