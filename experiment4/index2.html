Statement


INFINITY
This is an infinite oceans that generates an infinite sea-scape of boats and islands. You can also make a ripple effect when you click on water. You can use the arrow keys to scroll through the world.
VARIATION IN SPACE
Ocean depths are created with perlin noise, and the tiles tween up and down for a wave effect. The islands are created with a similar way with also boats placed on the ocean with hashmaps.
VARIATION IN KEY
Different noise generates significantly different terrain to make entirely new worlds and boat locations and shallow vs deep ocean transitions. There are collections of islands, big islands, and small islands
SIGNS OF LIFE
The ocean tiles all move up and down slowly using sine and time progression to simulate waves. There is also an option to click on a tile causing the surrounding tiles to splash up and down. There is also a day/night cycle that changes the background colour.
