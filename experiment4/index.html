Description

What are you trying to acomplish with this experiment?
I want to create an infinite world that needs to be uncovered through exploration and activity. I created this ocean that needs lit up by clicking on the lighthouses. The wave also have glimps of the moonlight.


Technical

Describe in detail what you did technically and how it works?
I used perlin noise to generate the shallow and deep ocean depths. The lighthouses when lit up shed light on the surrounding area. The light effect is calculated by the distance from the lighthouse and the angle of the tile. When the lighthouse is clicked, the light effect is calculated and the tile is lit up.

Statement


INFINITY
This is an infinite oceans that generates an infinite number of lighthouses that can be lit up.
VARIATION IN SPACE
Ocean depths are created with perlin noise.
VARIATION IN KEY
Different noise generates significantly different terrain to make entirely new worlds and boat locations and shallow vs deep ocean transitions.
SIGNS OF LIFE
The ocean tiles all move up and down slowly using sine and time progression to simulate waves. You can also click on the lighthouses to light them up.
